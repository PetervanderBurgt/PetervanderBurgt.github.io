About Myself

I am a Master student Embedded systems, with an interest in electronic projects and all things considering newer and upcoming technologies like Internet of Things and Machine Learning. Besides that I like to use technology in new and interesting ways to help create a better and more joyful world.

When I was younger I was already interested in technology and using exhisting things in new ways.
That's why in middle school I followed the Science, Engineering and Health track (Natuur en Techniek, Natuur en Gezonedheid).
Next to that, I followed Research and Design (Onderzoeken en Ontwerpen), which focusses on tackling a big project in a group in steps. First one does research, after which you set goals for the project, and finally you realise it.

The subject of Research and Design was a mayor factor in my choice for a Bachelor programme at the University of Twente.
There I graduated from Creative Technology, which is a multidisciplinary study that focuses on designing products with the user in mind. It consists of three mayor branches, which are Computer Science, Electrical Engineering and Industrial Design.
In this study I learned a lot, like for example brainstorming, goal-oriented design, circuit design, CAD Design, programming and user-centered design. After this study I wanted to focus more on the circuit design and programming side of the study and chose my master

At this moment I am following the Embedded Systems Masters Programme at the University of Twente.
Here I learn about Embedded Systems design, where hardware like FPGAs are programmed by using hardware description languages. Besides this, I followed subjects discussing Real-Time Systems, Data Science, Internet of Things, and Machine Learning.

In my free time I like to make electronic projects, to make my life or the life of others easier or are just fun.
Next to that, I'd like to go out on hikes and ride my sports bike.

University Projects

Low-Cost Autonomous Pyranometer (2020)

For my Bachelor Thesis, I designed a Low-Cost Autonomous Pyranometer.
This is a special type of weather measurement sensor, which can measure the incoming Solar Radiation. The final product used two low cost sensors, a microcontroller, and a machine learning algorithm to replicate results from a High end reference sensor. The entire bachelor thesis can be found here.

DiscoverJockey (Data Physicalisation 2018)

The goal of this project is to expand the musical horizon of festival visitors, such that they are encouraged to visit unknown artists playing at stages on festivals, in this case: Lowlands.
This can also be seen in the name of the installation. A play on words has been made by combining the musical element of a DJ (Disk Jockey) that is also found in the DJ-table of the installation, with the possibility for the user to discover new music.
The visitor can fill in their preferences, and the installation will generate a match that helps them to find new artists they might also like. The interactive visualisation not only helps to find known, but also new artists.
This will help the upcoming artists to get more attention for the music that they try to make known to the public. Next to that, the installation will serve as a diverse attraction in the midst of the festival stages.

Muser (Intelligent Interaction Design 2018)

A smart watch, to help people with PTSD that suffer from panic attacks. When someone suffers a panic attack, music therapy can be of help to calm down this person.
Together with a group of 6 people, I worked on making a smart watch which could detect a panic attack and when this was the case, administer music therapy to reconnect the two hemispheres of the brain, causing a calming effect on the person in question.

Faster than Light (Art Installation GOGBOT 2017)

Hauto (2019)

This is a project that I'd like to come back to.
The car is almost entirely laser cutted, except for the joints, which are tackled by using M3 screws.
There is a lot of friction in the back axle, making it difficult for the car to move at slow speeds.
Furthermore, the steering can be improved by integrating some kind of geometry steering like Ackermann steering.
Finally, the Motor controller can be improved, as the now used L298n, is not very efficient, and cant handle the current needed.
